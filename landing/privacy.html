Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Cortex ("we", "us", "our") operates the Cortex desktop application and the cortex.dev website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and payment information (processed securely by our payment provider).

Usage data. We collect anonymized usage metrics such as feature usage frequency, session duration, and error reports to improve the product. We do not collect or store your prompts, generated content, or AI conversation history on our servers.

Device information. We collect basic device information (operating system, app version) for compatibility and support purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Cortex service
  • To process your subscription and payments
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, account changes, security alerts)
  • To improve product performance and fix bugs
  • To provide customer support

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

All traffic between the Cortex application and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3. Prompt history and files stored locally on your device are encrypted using AES-256. We employ a zero-knowledge architecture for locally stored data, meaning we cannot access your prompts, conversations, or generated content.

4. Data Retention

Account information is retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g., financial records for tax compliance).

5. Third-Party Services

Cortex integrates with third-party AI model providers (such as OpenAI, Anthropic, Midjourney, and others) to deliver its functionality. When you use these models through Cortex, your prompts are sent to the respective providers according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these providers.

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your full credit card number on our servers.

6. Cookies

The cortex.dev website uses essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. We may use anonymized analytics (such as Plausible or similar privacy-focused tools) to understand website traffic.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

8. Children's Privacy

Cortex is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification at least 14 days before the changes take effect.
