Refund Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

We want you to feel confident trying Cortex. Our refund policy is straightforward and designed to be fair.

The short version

Your first 30 days are free. Cancel during the trial and you pay nothing. After the trial, if you're not satisfied within your first paid month, contact us for a full refund. No questions asked.

1. Free Trial

Every new Cortex account starts with a 30-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card is required to start the trial. If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be charged.

2. First Paid Month Guarantee

If you continue past the free trial and are not satisfied with Cortex during your first paid billing period, contact us within 30 days of your first charge and we will issue a full refund. No justification required.

3. Subsequent Months

After the first paid month, subscriptions are non-refundable for partially used billing periods. You can cancel at any time from your dashboard, and your access will continue until the end of the current billing period. No further charges will be made after cancellation.

4. How to Request a Refund

Email support@cortex.dev with the email address associated with your account. Refunds are typically processed within 5 business days and returned to your original payment method.

5. Exceptions

We reserve the right to deny refund requests in cases of clear abuse, such as: repeated signup-refund cycles, violation of our Terms of Service, or fraudulent activity.
